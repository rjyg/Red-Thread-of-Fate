The Red Thread of Fate

Two people connected by the red thread are destined lovers. The thread may stretch or tangle, but never break.

A young boy meets Yue Lao, the god of marriage and love, under the moonlight.

Yue Lao reveals the red thread of fate, connecting the boy to his destined wife.

The red thread leads to a young girl, unknown to the boy.

In his youthful naivety, the boy throws a rock at the girl and runs away.

Years later, the boy, now a young man, is set to be married.

On his wedding night, he discovers his wife to be a great beauty of the village.

He notices an adornment on her eyebrow.

She reveals that a boy once threw a rock at her, leaving a scar.

The red thread of fate had bound them all along, from childhood to this destined union.
